Interior Project

Wooded Gray

Transforming the apartment through furnishing and minimal structural changes. Dark wood serves as an accent within the monochromatic gray palette, highlighting the metallic furniture and its interplay with different materials.

此案透過家具配置與少量結構調整，重新轉化公寓的居住氛圍。深色木質在灰色單色系之中形成重點，襯托金屬家具與不同材質之間的細緻關係。

The design works with restraint, using a gray base to quiet the apartment while allowing wood, metal, fabric, and light to build contrast. Instead of changing the space dramatically, the project refines how each surface and object meets daily use.

設計以克制的方式處理空間，透過灰色基底降低視覺干擾，再以木質、金屬、布料與光線建立層次。它並非大幅改變格局，而是重新整理每個表面與物件進入日常生活的方式。

Dimensions
47 sqm
Approach
Furnishing and minimal structural changes
Palette
Monochromatic gray, dark wood, metallic accents
Spatial Narrative

Gray, wood, and metallic detail

Wooded Gray is shaped around the tension between quietness and emphasis. A monochromatic gray palette gives the compact apartment a consistent visual field, while dark wood creates depth and frames the presence of selected furniture pieces.

Wooded Gray 的重點在於安靜與強調之間的平衡。灰色單色系讓小坪數公寓形成一致的視覺背景，深色木質則帶入深度，並框定家具在空間中的存在感。

Metallic surfaces introduce a sharper reflection against the softer gray surroundings. Through these material contrasts, the project gives the apartment a more composed rhythm without relying on heavy construction.

金屬表面在柔和灰階之中帶來更清晰的反射與線條感。透過這些材質對比，空間在不依賴大量施工的情況下，建立出更完整且沉著的節奏。

Material Sequence

Small changes, stronger atmosphere

The apartment is transformed through precise additions rather than large gestures. Furniture, finish, and proportion are used to make the interior feel more intentional, with the dark wood accents grounding the cooler gray field.

此案透過精準的增補，而非大幅度的設計動作來轉化空間。家具、表面與比例共同讓室內感受更有意識，深色木質也讓偏冷的灰階基底變得更加穩定。

Across the rooms, material shifts create a quiet dialogue: matte and reflective, soft and rigid, gray and dark wood. These details support a compact home that feels calm, structured, and carefully edited.

在不同場景之間，材質轉換形成安靜的對話：霧面與反射、柔軟與剛硬、灰色與深木色。這些細節支撐出一個安定、有秩序且經過細緻整理的小坪數住宅。
