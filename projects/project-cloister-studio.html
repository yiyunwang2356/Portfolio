Interior Project

Cloister Studio

Cloister Studio centers around creating an open and flexible environment that seamlessly blends living and working functions. Through a series of sliding doors and carefully framed thresholds, the interior is able to shift between openness and privacy, allowing everyday life and focused work to coexist within one continuous spatial experience.

Cloister Studio 以開放且具彈性的空間概念為核心，將生活與工作機能自然整合。透過一系列滑門與清楚而細膩的空間界面，室內在開放與私密之間取得平衡，讓日常起居與專注工作得以在同一個連續的場域中共存。

Rather than dividing the home into rigidly separated rooms, the project proposes a softer spatial rhythm shaped by movement, light, and visual continuity. Each area remains connected to the next, while still allowing moments of retreat, concentration, and pause when needed.

設計並不以明確切割的房間來定義功能，而是透過動線、光線與視覺延續性建立更柔軟的空間節奏。不同區域彼此相連，卻仍能在需要時提供安靜停留、專注工作與適度退讓的使用狀態。

Spatial Narrative

Sliding thresholds and continuity

The project uses sliding doors not simply as partitions, but as adjustable thresholds that shape visibility, movement, and intimacy. By allowing rooms to open into one another, the design creates a sense of continuity that makes the compact interior feel more generous, fluid, and adaptable over the course of the day.

此案中的滑門不只是分隔工具，而是可調整的空間界面，回應視線、動線與親密尺度之間的關係。透過讓不同房間彼此展開、相互滲透，住宅在有限尺度中仍保有更寬鬆、流動且可隨時間轉換的空間感受。

This spatial flexibility supports multiple rhythms of living. Working, resting, gathering, and retreating are not strictly isolated, but arranged through subtle transitions that allow the home to respond to different uses without losing its calm identity.

這樣的彈性也讓空間能夠承接不同的生活節奏。工作、休息、交流與獨處並非被完全切開，而是透過細微轉場彼此串連，使住宅在回應多種使用情境的同時，仍維持安定而清楚的整體氣質。

Living and Working

Calm material rhythm

Material restraint plays an important role in shaping the atmosphere of Cloister Studio. Soft tones, controlled details, and a balanced distribution of light help the interior feel quiet and composed, allowing the focus to shift from decorative excess toward everyday use and spatial clarity.

材質的節制使用是 Cloister Studio 氛圍建立的重要部分。柔和的色調、收斂的細節與均衡的光線安排，讓室內維持安靜、穩定且有秩序的感受，使設計的重點回到日常使用與空間清晰度本身。

What emerges is an environment that feels both intimate and open: a compact home that supports concentration without becoming closed, and daily life without becoming visually fragmented. The project ultimately balances practicality with a subtle but memorable spatial identity.

最終形成的是一個同時具備親密感與開放感的居住環境：它能支撐專注工作，卻不顯封閉；能容納日常生活，卻不流於零碎。也因此，此案在實用需求與細膩而可被記憶的空間識別之間取得了平衡。
