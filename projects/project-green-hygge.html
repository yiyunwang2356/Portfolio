Interior Project

Green Hygge

Green Hygge presents a compact home shaped by a sculptural sense of livability, balancing minimalism with warmth. Rather than pursuing a cold or overly reduced aesthetic, the project builds its atmosphere through soft material contrast, quiet tonal layering, and a carefully measured sense of domestic comfort.

Green Hygge 以具有雕塑感的居住性作為空間核心，在簡約語言之中維持溫度與生活感。設計並不追求冷調或過度抽離的極簡，而是透過柔和的材質對比、安靜的色彩層次與節制而細膩的尺度安排，建立一種穩定且可被日常感受的住宅氛圍。

The dining area acts as the heart of the home, where a freeform wooden table is paired with an olive-green cabinet to create a subtle asymmetry within an otherwise composed interior. This offset softens the room’s underlying symmetry and introduces a more relaxed rhythm, allowing the space to feel intentional without becoming rigid.

餐廳是整個住宅的核心，一張自由曲線的木桌搭配橄欖綠櫃體，在整體沉穩的空間秩序中形成細緻的不對稱關係。這樣的偏移柔化了原有的對稱結構，也讓空間在保持清楚設計意識的同時，依然維持放鬆而自然的生活節奏。

Spatial Narrative

Warm minimalism in a compact home

What defines the project is the way softness is embedded into a restrained spatial language. The interior remains visually clean and composed, yet avoids feeling severe through muted green tones, warm wood surfaces, and a layout that allows the eye to move naturally from one area to another.

這個案子的特色，在於它如何把柔和感放進一個節制而清楚的空間語言中。整體室內維持乾淨、安定且有秩序的視覺感受，同時透過低彩度綠色、溫潤木質與自然流動的佈局，避免空間顯得過於冷硬或緊繃。

Instead of treating a small footprint as a limitation, the design turns compactness into an opportunity for calibration. Furniture, storage, and circulation are carefully arranged to preserve openness while still supporting the intimacy, practicality, and ease required in everyday living.

設計並未將小坪數視為限制，而是把它轉化為尺度精準安排的契機。家具、收納與動線之間經過細膩協調，使住宅在維持開放感的同時，也能承接日常居住所需要的親密性、實用性與舒適度。

Material Continuity

From dining table to side table

The freeform dining table and olive-green cabinet introduce a quiet sense of asymmetry that becomes central to the emotional tone of the home. Rather than standing out as isolated objects, they shape a more relaxed composition in which domestic use, visual balance, and warmth are held together in a subtle but memorable way.

自由形木桌與橄欖綠櫃體所形成的細微不對稱，是這個住宅氣氛中的關鍵之一。它們不是各自獨立的單品，而是共同建立出更放鬆的空間構圖，讓生活感、視覺平衡與溫度自然地被串連在一起。

This dining table also extends beyond the interior itself. The leftover solid wood cut from its production was later reused to create the Wooden Night Stand in the Re:Craft series, connecting the project to a broader approach of material continuity and reuse. In this way, Green Hygge becomes not only a residential interior, but also the starting point of a smaller furniture narrative shaped by the afterlife of material.

這張餐桌的意義也不只停留在室內空間本身。製作餐桌時切下的實木剩料，後續被延伸製作為 Re:Craft 系列中的 Wooden Night Stand，讓這個案子進一步連結到材料延續與再利用的設計思考。也因此，Green Hygge 不只是住宅空間作品，同時也是另一件家具作品生成的起點。

Related Object / Re:Craft

Wooden Night Stand

The solid wood used in this object comes from the leftover material cut during the making of the dining table in Green Hygge, extending the material story of the interior into a smaller everyday piece.

這件家具所使用的實木，來自 Green Hygge 餐桌製作時所切下的剩餘材料，讓住宅空間中的材質敘事延伸成另一件日常使用的家具。

Rather than treating the remaining wood as waste, the project repositions it as the starting point of a new object — one that carries the memory of the interior while entering a different scale of use.

它並不是單純將剩料再次利用，而是把原本屬於餐桌製作過程中的木料重新轉化，讓其在不同尺度中延續價值，也保留這個住宅案的材質記憶。
